Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQs page. Here you’ll find answers to the most common questions about our cooperative.

What is this website for?

This platform is designed to improve users' experience with easy access to our services, information, and tools in a simple, user-friendly environment.

Do I need an account to use the site?

Some features may require you to create an account. This allows you to manage your shop, track order status, and access personal information.

How do I contact support?

If you need assistance, you can reach out through our email: contact@GFLH.uk. We aim to respond and resolve all enquiries as quickly as possible.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect your information from breaches and malicious attacks.

Can I update my details?

Yes, you can update your personal information at any time through your account menu.

Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal data is handled responsibly.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and usage data when you interact with our website.

This data is used to improve our services and provide a better user experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Personalise your experience
  • Communicate important updates to suppliers

Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to safeguard your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information may only be shared with trusted third parties where necessary to operate our services or comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data at any time.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at any point.

Accessibility Standards

We strive to ensure our website meets recognised accessibility standards and provides an inclusive experience for everyone.

Accessibility Features

  • Readable fonts and colour contrast
  • Keyboard-friendly controls
  • Compatibility with multiple devices

Ongoing Improvements

We continuously review and improve our website to enhance accessibility and usability.

Feedback

If you experience any accessibility issues or have suggestions, please contact us.
